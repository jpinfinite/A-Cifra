What is GameFi and How Does it Work?

GameFi is a term that combines "gaming" and "finance," referring to games that use blockchain technology to create more immersive and transparent experiences. In this context, AAA games on blockchain are developed by renowned studios, such as Electronic Arts, Ubisoft, and Rockstar Games, which aim to create innovative and profitable gaming experiences using blockchain technology.

AAA games on blockchain work similarly to traditional games, but with some additional features:

* Tokenization: Games can include tokens that represent items or resources within the game, which can be bought or sold on exchange markets.
* Blockchain: Blockchain technology is used to record and store tokens, ensuring the security and transparency of transactions.
* Community Interaction: Games can include mechanisms for community interaction, such as challenges, contests, and rewards, which can be carried out using tokens.
