XRP needs a strategy in the style of Solana to remain competitive, says Ripple executive.

XRP needs an urgent message to remain competitive.

In the crypto market, the struggle for supremacy is constant. And, in this game of power, no network wants to be left behind. It is in this context that Ripple, one of the main companies in the sector, is making an appeal for its XRP currency to adapt to the needs of the market. And, to do this, it is looking at Solana as a model to follow.

Solana is one of the most prominent layer 1 networks in the crypto market, known for its advanced technology and developer tools. And it is precisely this combination that Ripple believes can be the key to XRP's success. "The technology and developer tools of the XRP Ledger can be inspired by Solana to remain competitive," says a Ripple executive.

This is a warning message for Ripple, which needs to adapt to the needs of the market to avoid being left behind. And it is precisely this competition that is making crypto networks more innovative and efficient. It is a constant game of power, and XRP needs to stay in the game to avoid losing its position in the market.

Context and details of the facts

Ripple, the company behind the XRP currency, is facing challenges to maintain its position in the crypto market. In a scenario where competition is fierce, Ripple needs to find a strategy to stand out and maintain its competitiveness. According to a company executive, Solana is an example of how Ripple can be inspired to improve its platform.

Solana is a blockchain that stands out for its speed and scalability, allowing transactions in seconds and processing capacity of thousands of transactions per second. This is possible thanks to its Proof of History (PoH) consensus technology, which makes the network more efficient and scalable. Ripple, on the other hand, has a consensus technology called Ripple Protocol, which is not as efficient as Solana's PoH.

Immediate consequences

The lack of an effective strategy can have negative consequences for Ripple and XRP. The market may react with a negative response, affecting the value of the currency. Additionally, competition may intensify, with other cryptocurrencies and blockchains standing out and attracting investors.

In simple terms, Solana can process transactions faster because its PoH consensus technology makes the network more efficient. This means that Solana can process more transactions in a given time, which is essential for a blockchain that aims to be scalable.
