Market Analysis is fundamental for making informed decisions. This guide explores on-chain analysis: key metrics for investors.

## Fundamentals of Analysis

### Why Analyze?

- **Timing**: Identify entry/exit moments
- **Risk**: Manage exposure
- **Opportunities**: Find good investments
- **Strategy**: Develop a trading plan

## Analysis Methodology

### Main Approaches

1. **Technical Analysis**: Charts and indicators
2. **Fundamental Analysis**: Intrinsic value
3. **On-Chain Analysis**: Blockchain data
4. **Sentiment Analysis**: Market psychology

## Key Indicators

### Important Metrics

- **Price**: Price action and trends
- **Volume**: Confirmation of movements
- **Volatility**: Risk and opportunity
- **Liquidity**: Ease of trading

## Analysis Tools

### Recommended Platforms

- **TradingView**: Advanced charts
- **Glassnode**: On-chain analysis
- **CoinGecko**: Market data
- **Messari**: Fundamental research

## Risk Management

### Fundamental Principles

- **Stop Loss**: Always define
- **Position Sizing**: Don't risk more than 2-5%
- **Diversification**: Don't put everything in one asset
- **Take Profit**: Realize partial profits

## Trading Psychology

### Mental Traps

- **FOMO**: Fear of Missing Out
- **FUD**: Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt
- **Overtrading**: Excessive trading
- **Revenge Trading**: Emotional trading

## Conclusion

Market analysis is a skill that develops with continuous practice and study. Use multiple tools and always manage risk.
