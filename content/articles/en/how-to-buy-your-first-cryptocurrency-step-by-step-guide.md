Buying [your](/artigo/how-to-create-and-sell-your-first-nft-the-complete-guide "How to Create and Sell Your First NFT: The Complete Guide") **first cryptocurrency** may seem complicated, but this step-by-step guide will simplify the entire process.

## Step 1: Choose an exchange

**Recommended Exchanges in Brazil:**
- Binance
- Mercado Bitcoin
- Foxbit
- NovaDAX

**Selection Criteria:**
- Security and reputation
- Competitive fees
- Ease of use
- Portuguese support

## Step 2: Registration

1. Access the exchange website
2. Click on "Register" or "Create Account"
3. Fill in: email, strong password
4. Confirm the email

## Step 3: KYC Verification

**Required Documents:**
- RG or CNH
- Proof of residence
- Selfie

**Process:**
1. Access "Identity Verification"
2. Send photos of the documents
3. Wait for approval (1-24 hours)

## Step 4: Deposit

**Methods:**
- PIX (faster)
- TED/DOC
- Credit card (higher fees)

**How to Do It:**
1. Go to "Deposit" or "Wallet"
2. Choose "BRL" or "Real"
3. Select method (PIX recommended)
4. Follow instructions
5. Wait for confirmation

## Step 5: Purchase

**Two Forms:**

### Simple Purchase (Recommended)
1. Go to "Buy Crypto"
2. Choose the currency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
3. Enter the value in R$
4. Confirm the purchase

### Advanced Purchase (Trading)
1. Access "Trade" or "Negotiate"
2. Choose the pair (ex: BTC/BRL)
3. Select "Market" (immediate purchase)
4. Enter quantity
5. Confirm

## Step 6: Storage

**Options:**

### Leave on Exchange
- Easy for trading
- Less secure

### Transfer to Wallet
- You control the keys
- More secure
- Recommended for high values

## Important Tips

1. **Start Small**: Test with R$ 50-100
2. **Use 2FA**: Activate two-factor authentication
3. **Be Careful with Fees**: Compare before buying
4. **Don't Invest Everything**: Diversify
5. **Study Before**: Understand what you're buying

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Buying without researching
- Investing money that needs it
- Not activating security
- Falling for "double your money" scams
- Selling in panic

## Next Steps

After buying your first crypto:
1. Learn about wallets
2. Study the market
3. Consider DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)
4. Never stop learning
