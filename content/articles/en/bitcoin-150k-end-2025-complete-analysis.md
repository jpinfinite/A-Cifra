### 2. 2024 Halving: Effects Still Ongoing

Bitcoin's fourth halving occurred in April 2024, reducing miner rewards from 6.25 BTC to 3.125 BTC per block.

**Historical Halving Pattern:**
- **2012**: 9,000% increase in the following 18 months
- **2016**: 2,900% increase in the following 18 months
- **2020**: 700% increase in the following 18 months
- **2024**: We're in the 19th month post-halving

:::warning Attention
