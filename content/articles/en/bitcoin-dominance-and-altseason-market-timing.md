## Fundamentals of Analysis

### Why Analyze?

- **Timing**: Identify entry/exit moments
- **Risk**: Manage exposure
- **Opportunities**: Find good investments
- **Strategy**: Develop a trading plan

## Analysis Methodology

### Main Approaches

1. **Technical Analysis**: Charts and indicators
2. **Fundamental Analysis**: Intrinsic value
3. **On-Chain Analysis**: Blockchain data
4. **Sentiment Analysis**: Market psychology

## Key Indicators

### Important Metrics

- **Price**: Price action and trends
- **Volume**: Confirmation of movements
- **Volatility**: Risk and opportunity
- **Liquidity**: Ease of trading

- **Glassnode**: On-chain analysis

- **Messari**: Fundamental research
