Yield Farming is one of the most popular ways to earn passive income in [defi](/artigo/defi-yield-farming-protocolos-seguros-2026), but it requires knowledge and risk management.

## What is Yield Farming?

Yield farming is the process of lending or providing liquidity to [defi](/artigo/defi-yield-farming-protocolos-seguros-2026) protocols in exchange for rewards, usually in the form of tokens.

## How Does it Work?

1. **Provide Liquidity**: Deposit tokens in a pool
2. **Receive LP Tokens**: Proof of participation
3. **Stake LP Tokens**: In farms for rewards
4. **Earn Tokens**: Rewards from the protocol

## Important Metrics

### APY vs APR
- **APR**: Simple annual rate
- **APY**: Includes compounding
- APY is always higher than APR

### TVL (Total Value Locked)
- Total liquidity in the protocol
- Higher TVL = generally safer

### Impermanent Loss
- Loss when providing liquidity
- Occurs when prices diverge
- Can be compensated by rewards

## Farming Strategies

### 1. Stablecoin Farming
- Lower risk
- APY: 5-20%
- No impermanent loss
- Example: USDC-USDT

### 2. Blue Chip Farming
- Medium risk
- APY: 20-50%
- Established tokens
- Example: ETH-USDC

### 3. High Risk Farming
- High risk
- APY: 100-1000%+
- New tokens
- Risk of rug pull

## Calculating Returns

**Example:**
- Investment: $1,000
- APY: 100%
- Period: 1 year
- Return: $1,000

**With Daily Compounding:**
- Final return: ~$1,700

## Risks of Yield Farming

❌ **Smart Contract Risk**
- Bugs in the code
- Hacks

❌ **Impermanent Loss**
- Price variation
- Can exceed gains

❌ **Rug Pull**
- Developers flee
- Project abandons

❌ **Volatility**
- Token prices drop
- Rewards lose value

## Getting Started

1. **Study the Protocol**
   - Audit?
   - TVL?
   - Reputation?

2. **Start Small**
   - Test with $100-500
   - Understand the process

3. **Diversify**
   - Don't put all in one farm
   - Multiple protocols

4. **Monitor**
   - APY changes constantly
   - Rebalance when necessary

5. **Reinvest**
   - Compound rewards
   - Maximize gains
