The Uniswap is the world's largest decentralized exchange (DEX). This tutorial teaches you how to use the platform step by step.

## What You Need

- MetaMask wallet installed
- ETH for gas fees
- Tokens to exchange

## Step 1: Access Uniswap

1. Go to app.uniswap.org
2. Click on "Connect Wallet"
3. Select MetaMask
4. Approve the connection

## Step 2: Make a Swap

1. Select the token you have (e.g. ETH)
2. Select the token you want (e.g. USDC)
3. Enter the amount
4. Check the fee and slippage
5. Click on "Swap"
6. Confirm on MetaMask

## Understanding Slippage

- Difference between expected and executed price
- Recommended: 0.5-1%
- Less liquid tokens: up to 3%

## Gas Fees

- Vary according to congestion
- Use gwei tracker to save
- Avoid peak hours

## Security Tips

✅ Always check the token address
✅ Use suitable slippage
✅ Start with small values
❌ Never share your seed phrase

You're done! You now know how to use Uniswap.
