What is Real Yield and How Does it Work?

Real Yield is an innovative concept in the DeFi world that promises to revolutionize the way investors manage their assets. In short, Real Yield is a mechanism that allows investors to receive a real interest rate, unlinked from inflation, when lending assets on DeFi platforms.

The idea behind Real Yield is simple: when lending assets on DeFi platforms, investors receive an interest rate in the form of tokens, which can be sold on the market to generate revenue. However, these tokens can lose value due to inflation, reducing the real return for investors.

Real Yield solves this problem by creating a mechanism that adjusts the interest rate based on inflation. This is done through a mathematical formula that takes into account the inflation rate and the nominal interest rate. The result is a real interest rate that is unlinked from inflation, ensuring that investors receive a real return.
