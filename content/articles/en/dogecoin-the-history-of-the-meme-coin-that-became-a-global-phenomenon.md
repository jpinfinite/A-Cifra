## The Origin (2013)

Created by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer as a satire of [bitcoin](/artigo/bitcoin-200k-2026-previsao-institucional), Dogecoin used the viral Shiba Inu meme at the time.

**Original Goal:**
- Be a "fun" cryptocurrency
- More accessible than [bitcoin](/artigo/bitcoin-200k-2026-previsao-institucional)
- Friendly community

## The Rise

### 2014-2020: Strong Community
- Funded the Jamaican Olympic team
- Sponsored a NASCAR car
- Active community on Reddit

### 2021: The Year of DOGE
- Elon Musk started tweeting about DOGE
- Over 12,000% valuation
- Reached top 10 cryptocurrencies

- **Supply**: Unlimited (5 billion/year)
- **Block Time**: 1 minute
