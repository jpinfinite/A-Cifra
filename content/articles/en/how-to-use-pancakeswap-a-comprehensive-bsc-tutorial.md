The [PancakeSwap](/artigo/como-usar-pancakeswap-tutorial "Como Usar o PancakeSwap: Tutorial Completo na BSC") is the largest DEX on the Binance Smart Chain. Learn to use all the features.

## Configure MetaMask for BSC

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click on the network (top)
3. "Add Network"
4. Fill in:
   - Name: BSC Mainnet
   - RPC: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
   - Chain ID: 56
   - Symbol: BNB
5. Save

## Step 1: Connect Wallet

1. Access pancakeswap.finance
2. Click on "Connect Wallet"
3. Select MetaMask
4. Approve connection

## Step 2: Make Swap

1. Click on "Trade" > "Swap"
2. Select source token (ex: BNB)
3. Select destination token (ex: CAKE)
4. Enter quantity
5. Adjust slippage if necessary
6. Click on "Swap"
7. Confirm in MetaMask

## Step 3: Add Liquidity

1. Go to "Liquidity"
2. Click on "Add Liquidity"
3. Choose token pair
4. Enter quantities
5. Approve tokens
6. Confirm addition
7. Receive LP tokens

## Step 4: Farming

1. Go to "Farms"
2. Choose farm (ex: CAKE-BNB)
3. Click on "Enable"
4. Stake your LP tokens
5. Earn CAKE as reward

## Step 5: Stake CAKE

1. Go to "Pools"
2. Choose CAKE pool
3. Click on "Enable"
4. Stake CAKE
5. Earn more CAKE

## Fees

- Swap: 0.25%
- Farming: Free
- Gas fees: ~$0.10-0.50

## Tips

✅ Start with small values
✅ Understand impermanent loss
✅ Compare APRs
✅ Reinvest rewards
