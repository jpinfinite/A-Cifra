**What is Bitcoin and How Does it Work?**

Bitcoin is a decentralized cryptocurrency based on blockchain technology that allows for secure and fast transactions between users without the need for intermediaries. It works on a network of distributed computers that record and validate transactions in a public blockchain.

**How Does Bitcoin Work?**

1. **Mining**: The mining process is responsible for validating transactions and adding new blocks to the blockchain. This is done by specialized computers that solve complex mathematical problems, which confers significant computational power.
2. **Blockchain**: The blockchain is a list of blocks, each containing information about the transactions made. It is public and can be viewed by anyone.
3. **Transactions**: Transactions are made between users and are validated by the network of distributed computers.
4. **Cryptography**: Transactions are encrypted to ensure the security and privacy of users.
