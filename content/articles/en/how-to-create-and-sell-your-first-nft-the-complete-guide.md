Create and sell **NFTs** has never been easier. This guide shows the [complete](/artigo/how-to-use-pancakeswap-a-complete-tutorial-on-bsc "How to Use PancakeSwap: A Complete Tutorial on BSC") process on OpenSea.

## What You Need

- Digital art (image, video, audio)
- MetaMask wallet
- ETH or Polygon for fees

## Step 1: Prepare Your Art

**Accepted Formats:**
- Images: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Videos: MP4
- Audio: MP3
- 3D: GLB

**Size:** Maximum 100MB

## Step 2: Configure MetaMask

1. Install MetaMask
2. Create wallet
3. Save seed phrase
4. Add ETH or Polygon

## Step 3: Access OpenSea

1. Go to opensea.io
2. Click on "Create"
3. Connect MetaMask
4. Approve connection

## Step 4: Create Collection

1. Click on "My Collections"
2. "Create a collection"
3. Fill in:
   - Name
   - Description
   - Logo
   - Banner
4. Choose blockchain (ethereum or Polygon)

## Step 5: Mint NFT

1. Click on "Create"
2. Upload art
3. Fill in:
   - NFT name
   - Description
   - Properties (optional)
   - Royalties (0-10%)
4. Choose collection
5. Click on "Create"

**Cost:**
- Polygon: Free
- ethereum: Gas fee

## Step 6: List for Sale

1. Go to your NFT
2. Click on "Sell"
3. Choose type:
   - Fixed Price (fixed price)
   - Auction (auction)
4. Set price
5. Set duration
6. Confirm listing

Creating NFTs is simple, but selling requires marketing and community. Start on Polygon to test without costs.
