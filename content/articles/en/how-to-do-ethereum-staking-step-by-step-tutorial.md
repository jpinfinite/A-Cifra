Making **staking** of **ethereum** allows you to earn passive rewards while helping to protect the network. See how to get started.

## Staking Options

### 1. Solo Staking (32 ETH)
- Requires 32 ETH
- You run a validator
- Maximum control and rewards
- Technical

### 2. Staking in Pool
- Any amount
- Easier
- Service fees
- Recommended for most

### 3. Liquid Staking
- Receives liquid token (stETH, rETH)
- Can use in defi
- Flexibility

## Recommended Platforms

**Lido Finance**
- Most popular
- Receives stETH
- Fee: 10%

**Rocket Pool**
- Decentralized
- Receives rETH
- Minimum: 0.01 ETH

**Coinbase/Binance**
- Easy for beginners
- Custodial
- Variable fees

## Tutorial: Staking on Lido

1. Access lido.fi
2. Connect MetaMask
3. Enter amount of ETH
4. Click "Stake"
5. Confirm transaction
6. Receive stETH

## Returns

- Average APR: 3-5%
- Varies with network
- Rewards in ETH
