What is Crédito On-Chain and How Does it Work?

Crédito On-Chain is a type of decentralized lending that uses blockchain technology to facilitate the granting and repayment of loans. This means that loans are recorded and managed on a blockchain, allowing for unparalleled transparency and security.

The Crédito On-Chain process is as follows:

1. **Creditor and Debtor Registration**: Creditors and debtors register on a Crédito On-Chain platform, providing information about their identities and credit histories.
2. **Credit Evaluation**: The platform uses artificial intelligence algorithms to evaluate the debtor's creditworthiness and determine the amount of loan that can be granted.
3. **Loan Granting**: The creditor grants the loan to the debtor, which is recorded on a blockchain.
4. **Interest Payment**: The debtor pays interest to the creditor, which is also recorded on a blockchain.
