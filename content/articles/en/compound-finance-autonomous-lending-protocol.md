## How It Works?

### Supply (Lend)
- Deposit cryptocurrencies
- Receive cTokens (e.g. cUSDC)
- Earn interest automatically
- Withdraw at any time

### Borrow (Take a Loan)
- Use crypto as collateral
- Take a loan of other assets
- Pay variable interest
- Maintain collateralization ratio
