How to Buy Fan [Tokens](/artigo/como-comprar-fan-tokens-guia-iniciantes "Como Comprar Fan Tokens: Guia Passo a Passo para Iniciantes"): A Step-by-Step Guide for Beginners

Fan [Tokens](/artigo/cmo-comprar-fan-tokens-gua-paso-a-paso-para-iniciantes "Cómo Comprar Fan Tokens: Guía Paso a Paso para Iniciantes") have become a fever in the world of sports. They allow fans not only to invest but also to actively participate in the decisions of their favorite clubs, such as choosing the stadium music or the uniform design.

If you read our article about the [2025 Club World Cup and the Fan Token Revolution](/article/club-world-cup-2025-fan-tokens), you know about the potential of this market. Now, let's get to the practice: how to buy your first Fan Token?

## What You Need to Know Before You Start

Fan [Tokens](/artigo/cmo-comprar-fan-tokens-gua-paso-a-paso-para-principiantes "Cómo Comprar Fan Tokens: Guía Paso a Paso para Principiantes") are digital assets (cryptocurrencies) that run on a blockchain (usually the Chiliz Chain). To buy them, you need an account on an exchange or the official Socios.com app.

### Step 1: Choose Your Exchange

The main platforms for buying Fan Tokens in Brazil are:

1.  **Binance:** Highest liquidity and variety of tokens (Santos, Lazio, Porto, Alpine).
2.  **Mercado Bitcoin:** Strong presence of Brazilian tokens (Vasco, Corinthians).
3.  **Socios.com:** The native app of Chiliz, focused on utility and voting.

### Step 2: Open Your Account and Deposit Real Money

The process is simple and regulated:

1.  Download the app of the exchange you chose.
2.  Register (KYC) by sending a photo of your ID and a selfie.
3.  Deposit Real Money via **Pix**. The money usually arrives immediately.

### Step 3: Find the Token of Your Team

In the search bar of the exchange, type the name of the team or the token abbreviation.

*   **Flamengo:** $MENGO
*   **Corinthians:** $SCCP
*   **São Paulo:** $SPFC
*   **Paris Saint-Germain:** $PSG
*   **Barcelona:** $BAR

### Step 4: Make the Purchase

1.  Select the trading pair (e.g., MENGO/BRL or MENGO/USDT).
2.  Choose the order type:
    *   **Market (Market):** Buy at the current best price available.
    *   **Limit (Limit):** You set the maximum price you want to pay.
3.  Enter the quantity and confirm.

You're done! The tokens are now in your exchange wallet.

## Golden Tip: Participate!

The fun of Fan Tokens isn't just the financial gain, but the utility. If you bought through Socios.com, stay tuned for polls. If you bought on another exchange, consider transferring to the Socios.com app to vote.

## Risks and Precautions

As with any cryptocurrency, Fan Tokens are volatile. The price can rise sharply on winning days or fall on losing days. Invest with caution and never put your rent money at risk.
