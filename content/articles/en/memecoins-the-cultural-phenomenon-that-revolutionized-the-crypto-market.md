What are Memecoins?

[Memecoins](/artigo/memecoins-el-fenmeno-cultural-que-revolucion-el-mercado-cripto "Memecoins: El Fenómeno Cultural que Revolucionó el Mercado Cripto") are cryptocurrencies inspired by internet memes, popular characters, or viral jokes. Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, which were created with specific technical purposes, memecoins were born mainly as entertainment or satire of the crypto market.

Characteristics of Memecoins

- **Cultural Origin:** Based on memes, characters, or popular jokes
- **Strong Community:** Driven by engaged and loyal communities
- **Viral Marketing:** Growth through social media and influencers
- **High Volatility:** Extremely volatile and unpredictable prices
- **Abundant Supply:** Generally have trillions of tokens in circulation
- **Low Unit Price:** Fractional prices that attract small investors

History of Memecoins

The history of [memecoins](/artigo/memecoins-fenomeno-cultural "Memecoins: O Fenômeno Cultural que Revolucionou o Mercado Cripto") began in 2013 with Dogecoin, but exploded in popularity during the 2021 bull market.

Main Memecoins in the Market

1. Dogecoin (DOGE) - The Pioneer
- Market Cap among the top 10 cryptocurrencies
- First memecoin, established community
- Support from Elon Musk and acceptance by companies

2. Shiba Inu (SHIB) - The "Dogecoin Killer"
- Launched in 2020 as an alternative to Dogecoin
- Complete ecosystem: ShibaSwap, NFTs, metaverse
- "Shib Army" extremely engaged

3. Pepe (PEPE) - The Classic Meme
- Based on the Pepe the Frog meme
- Launched in 2023 with explosive growth
- No declared utility, pure meme

How to Invest with Safety

1. Research and Due Diligence
- Verify the development team
- Analyze the project's roadmap
- Evaluate the community (genuine vs bots)
- Confirm contract audits

2. Risk Management
- **Maximum 5-10%** of the portfolio in memecoins
- Never invest money that cannot be lost
- Use stop loss and take profit
- Diversify among different projects

3. Where to Buy
- **Centralized Exchanges:** Binance, Coinbase, KuCoin
- **DEXs:** Uniswap, PancakeSwap, SushiSwap
- **Wallets:** MetaMask, Trust Wallet

Risks

Risks are inherent in investing in memecoins.
