Choosing the right digital [wallet](/artigo/cold-wallet-vs-hot-wallet-seguranca "Cold Wallet vs Hot Wallet: Qual é Mais Segura?") is fundamental for the security of your cryptocurrencies. There are various types, each with its specific characteristics.

## What is a Digital Wallet?

A cryptocurrency [wallet](/artigo/cold-wallet-vs-hot-wallet---definitive-crypto-security-guide-2025 "Cold Wallet vs Hot Wallet - Definitive Crypto Security Guide 2025") stores your private keys - codes that give access to your assets on the blockchain. It does not store the coins themselves, but the keys to access them.

## Types of Wallets

### 1. Hot Wallets (Hot Wallets)

**Characteristics:**
- Connected to the internet
- Fast and convenient access
- Ideal for daily use

**Examples:**
- MetaMask
- Trust Wallet
- Exodus
- Exchange wallets

**Advantages:**
✅ Easy access
✅ Fast transactions
✅ Free (most)

**Disadvantages:**
❌ Vulnerable to hacks
❌ Depends on internet
❌ Less secure

### 2. Cold Wallets (Cold Wallets)

**Characteristics:**
- Offline
- Maximum security
- Ideal for long-term storage

**Types:**

#### Hardware Wallets
- Ledger Nano S/X
- Trezor
- SafePal

**Advantages:**
✅ Maximum security
✅ Protection against malware
✅ Physical backup

**Disadvantages:**
❌ Initial cost (R$ 300-1000)
❌ Less convenient
❌ Can be lost/damaged

#### Paper Wallets
- Printed keys on paper
- Completely offline
- Free

**Advantages:**
✅ Totally offline
✅ No cost
✅ Immune to digital hacks

**Disadvantages:**
❌ Can be lost/damaged
❌ Difficult to use
❌ Not recommended for beginners

### 3. Custodial Wallets

**Characteristics:**
- Third parties hold your keys
- Exchanges like Binance, Coinbase

**Advantages:**
✅ Easy password recovery
✅ Customer support
✅ Convenient

**Disadvantages:**
❌ You do not control the keys
❌ Risk of exchange hack
❌ May have restrictions

## How to Choose?

### For Beginners:
- **Reliable Hot [Wallet](/artigo/cold-wallet-vs-hot-wallet---the-definitive-crypto-security-guide-2025 "Cold Wallet vs Hot Wallet - The Definitive Crypto Security Guide 2025")** (MetaMask, Trust Wallet)
- Small amounts
- Frequent use

### For Investors:
- **Hardware Wallet** for large amounts
- Hot wallet for daily use
- Diversification of wallets

### For Traders:
- Exchange wallet for trading
- Hardware wallet for storage
- Hot wallet for fast transactions

## Best Security Practices

1. **Never share your seed phrase**
2. **Use two-factor authentication (2FA)**
3. **Make a backup of your keys**
4. **Test with small amounts first**
5. **Keep software up-to-date**
6. **Use wallets from trusted sources**

## Conclusion

The choice of wallet depends on your profile:
- **Maximum security**: Hardware wallet
- **Convenience**: Hot wallet
- **Balance**: Combination of both

Remember: "Not your keys, not your coins" - if you do not control the keys, you do not control the coins.
