## Catalysts of the Rally

### 1. Resolution of the SEC Case

**Context:** 4-year legal battle (2020-2024)

**Timeline:**
- **2020:** SEC sues [Ripple](/artigo/ripple-and-xrp-revolutionizing-global-payments "Ripple and XRP: Revolutionizing Global Payments") for selling "unregistered security"
- **2023:** Ripple wins partial victories
- **2024:** Final agreement - [Ripple](/artigo/xrp-y-ripple-revolucionando-pagos-globales "XRP y Ripple: Revolucionando Pagos Globales") pays $125M (vs $2B requested)
- **Nov 2025:** Complete regulatory clarity

**Impact:** XRP is officially **NOT a security** for programmatic sales.

### 2. Relisting on American Exchanges

**Coinbase:** Relisted XRP on 15 Nov
**Kraken:** Relisted on 18 Nov
**Gemini:** Relisted on 22 Nov
**Binance.US:** Relisted on 25 Nov

**Impact:** Access for 50+ million American users.

### 3. Expanding Banking Partnerships

**RippleNet:** 300+ financial institutions

**New Partnerships (November):**
- **JPMorgan:** Pilot test with JPM Coin + XRP
- **Bank of America:** Exploring cross-border payments
- **Santander:** Expanding to Latin America
- **SBI Holdings:** Launching in Japan

### 4. Launch of RLUSD

**RLUSD:** Stablecoin backed by the US dollar, approved by the NYDFS

**Launch:** 4 December (next week)

**Impact:** XRP will be used for RLUSD liquidity.
