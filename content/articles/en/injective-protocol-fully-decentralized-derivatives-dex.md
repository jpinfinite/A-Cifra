Injective Protocol is a Layer 1 blockchain built specifically for decentralized financial applications.

## Differentials

### Decentralized Order Book

Unlike AMMs, Injective uses on-chain order book for better price discovery.

### Zero Gas Fees

Transactions with no gas fees for traders.

### Cross-Chain

Native support for multiple blockchain assets.

## Products

### Spot Trading

Spot token trading with order book.

### Perpetuals

Perpetual contracts with up to 20x leverage.

### Futures

Futures contracts with physical settlement.

### Options

Decentralized options trading.

## INJ Token

- **Staking**: 15-20% APY
- **Governance**: Voting on proposals
- **Fee Burning**: Deflationary
- **Collateral**: Margin trading

## Ecosystem

- **Helix**: Main DEX
- **Mito**: Launchpad
- **Hydro**: Market maker

Injective is bringing traditional finance sophistication to DeFi in a fully decentralized manner.
