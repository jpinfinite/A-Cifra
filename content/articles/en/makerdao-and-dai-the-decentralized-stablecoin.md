MakerDAO is the protocol that created DAI, the most important decentralized stablecoin in the [defi](/artigo/defi-yield-farming-protocolos-seguros-2026) ecosystem.

## What is DAI?

Stablecoin pegged to the dollar ($1):
- Decentralized
- Collateralized by crypto
- No custody
- Censorship-resistant

## How is DAI Created?

### Vaults (CDPs)

1. Deposit collateral (ETH, WBTC, etc.)
2. Generate DAI (up to 66% of the value)
3. Pay stability fee
4. Return DAI to release collateral

Example:
- Deposit: $10,000 in ETH
- Generate: $6,600 in DAI
- Fee: 2-5% per year

## Why Use DAI?

Decentralized
Transparent
Censorship-resistant
Accepted everywhere [defi](/artigo/defi-yield-farming-protocolos-seguros-2026)

## Governance (MKR)

MKR token controls:
- Stability fees
- Collateral types
- Risk parameters

## Risks

Liquidation if collateral falls
Complexity
Gas fees

## Conclusion

DAI is the most decentralized and fundamental stablecoin for [defi](/artigo/defi-yield-farming-protocolos-seguros-2026). Ideal for those who value decentralization.
