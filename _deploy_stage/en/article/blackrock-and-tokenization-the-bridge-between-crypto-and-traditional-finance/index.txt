**How Does Tokenization Work?**

The tokenization process:

1. Real-world asset (e.g., a $10 million building)
2. Legal structuring (SPV, trust, etc.)
3. Token issuance (10 million tokens at $1 each)
4. Registration on blockchain ([ethereum](/artigo/staking-ethereum-guia-renda-passiva-2026), Polygon, etc.)
5. Distribution (sale to investors)
6. Secondary market trading (exchanges, DEXs)

**Advantages of Tokenization**

1. Fractionation: Expensive assets become accessible
2. Liquidity: Illiquid assets become liquid
3. Transparency: Ownership registered on blockchain
4. Efficiency: Lower transaction costs
5. Programmability: Smart contracts automate processes
