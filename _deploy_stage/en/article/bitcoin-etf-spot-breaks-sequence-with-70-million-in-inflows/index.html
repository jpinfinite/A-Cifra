Bitcoin ETF Spot Breaks Sequence of Exits with $70 Million in Inflows

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is $70M a lot or a little?

Moderate. Strong days have $200M-$500M. Breaking the sequence of exits is positive.

2. Does this guarantee Bitcoin will reach $100K?

No. But it's a bullish sign. Institutional flows usually precede price increases.

3. Why is Grayscale still showing outflows?

High fees (1.5%). Investors migrating to cheaper ETFs (0.2-0.25%).

4. Should I buy an ETF or Bitcoin directly?

ETF: Easier, regulated, in investment account. Bitcoin directly: You control the keys, no annual fees.

5. Which ETF is better?

BlackRock IBIT or Fidelity FBTC (both 0.25% fee, high liquidity).

6. Do ETF inflows affect the price?

Yes. ETFs need to buy Bitcoin on the spot market when there are inflows.

7. Will December be bullish?

Probably (60% chance). December historically strong + positive momentum.

8. What if there are more outflows?

Correction to $85K-$90K is possible. But long-term trend remains bullish.

9. Are institutions selling?

No. Outflows were temporary realization of profits. Trend is accumulation.

10. Is it worth investing now?

Yes, if you have a 6+ month horizon. Short-term may have volatility.
