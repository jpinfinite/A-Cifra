Yield Farming: Complete Guide to Maximizing Returns in DeFi

Learn everything about yield farming: what it is, how it works, best strategies, risks, and how to start earning passive income in DeFi.

By Jonatha PereiraOctober 30, 2025

Yield Farming is one of the most popular ways to earn passive income in defi, but it requires knowledge and risk management.

What is Yield Farming?

Yield farming is the process of lending or providing liquidity to defi protocols in exchange for rewards, usually in the form of tokens.

How Does it Work?

  1. Provide Liquidity: Deposit tokens in a pool
  2. Receive LP Tokens: Proof of participation
  3. Stake LP Tokens: In farms for rewards
  4. Earn Tokens: Rewards from the protocol

Important Metrics

APY vs APR

  • APR: Simple annual rate
  • APY: Includes compounding
  • APY is always higher than APR

TVL (Total Value Locked)

  • Total liquidity in the protocol
  • Higher TVL = generally safer

Impermanent Loss

  • Loss when providing liquidity
  • Occurs when prices diverge
  • Can be compensated by rewards

Getting Started

  1. Study the Protocol

    • Audit?
    • TVL?
    • Reputation?

  2. Start Small

    • Test with $100-500
    • Understand the process

  3. Diversify

    • Don't put all in one farm
    • Multiple protocols

  4. Monitor

    • APY changes constantly
    • Rebalance when necessary

  5. Reinvest

    • Compound rewards
    • Maximize gains

For beginners:

  1. Use stablecoins
  2. Audited protocols
  3. High TVL (>$100M)

Conclusion

Yield farming can generate good returns, but requires:

  • Technical knowledge
  • Active management
  • Risk tolerance
  • Capital for gas fees

Start small, learn, and scale gradually.
